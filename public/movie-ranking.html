Best Movies according to Charlie

A list of movies I liked over the past months

Everything, Everywhere, All at Once

Multi-verse family drama with great music, exploration of Chinese-American family dynamics and killer martial arts. And Michelle Yeoh, of course.

Portrait of a Lady on Fire

French lesbian period drama with beautiful cinematography and an incredible ending.

La tortue rouge

Completely dialogue-free animation film, portraying emotions in a very different way.
